About me

In life, there is not much opportunities for many people, so take what others offer and don't take things for granted. Small opportunities are often the beginning of great enterprises. If I want to lead my life successfully and happily, be alert and prepared to hear the call of the opportunity that may come at any moment. My journey began when my ex-university architect friend of mine invited me in a WhatsApp group. I saw a friend of mine who lives in America posting a lot of good graphic design, she motivated me to start learning about 'how to make a good presentation posting'. I started to learn graphic design on my own from my mobile phone using numerous free apps such as snapseed to Adobe Creative Cloud Express. In two years time, I made more than one thousand designs and I always enjoy spending my time designing. My productivity for designing made me feel the need to get more exposure, and that's why I chose to learn web development. In order to display my design online and garner more public interest.
